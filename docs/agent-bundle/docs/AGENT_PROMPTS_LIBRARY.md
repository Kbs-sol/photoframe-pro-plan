# Prompts Library — 20 Ready-to-Paste Session Starters

Copy-paste any of these into your chat tool. The agent's session-open protocol handles the rest.

## Daily rhythm

1. **Standard daily**
   > Run today's ChitraFrame session.

2. **Quick pulse (5 min)**
   > Pulse check only: pull GSC + Razorpay last 3 days, tell me if anything moved. No PRs.

3. **After a bad day**
   > Yesterday's orders dropped. Pull GA4 funnel + GSC top-losing pages for the last 7 days. Diagnose. Propose max 2 PRs.

## SEO-focused

4. **CTR rescue**
   > Find every page with >200 impressions and <2% CTR in the last 14 days. Rewrite title + meta. Max 5 PRs.

5. **Page-2 push**
   > Find every keyword we rank at positions 11–20 with volume >100. Propose the single-highest-leverage content addition for each. Max 3 PRs.

6. **Content gap sweep**
   > Find every GSC query with >50 impressions and no matching page. Cluster into blog topics. Propose 3 blog outlines. Draft the top one.

7. **Internal linking pass**
   > For every product page in Devotional + Automotive categories, propose 2 outbound internal links each. One PR total.

## Content

8. **Festival landing page**
   > Diwali is 90 days out. Draft a Diwali landing page: 6 curated products, SEO copy, gift-wrap upsell, FAQ. Open as PR.

9. **Blog post — targeted**
   > Draft one blog post targeting "framed prints for pooja room". Follow blog-post.md. Publish PR.

10. **Blog post — from data**
    > Look at GSC queries with impressions >30 and no page. Pick the highest-intent one. Draft a blog post targeting it.

## Conversion

11. **PDP conversion audit**
    > For every PDP with sessions >100 and add-to-cart rate <3%, propose one specific change (hero, headline, or size selector copy). Max 4 PRs.

12. **Abandoned cart pattern**
    > GA4 checkout funnel — where's the biggest drop? Diagnose. Propose one fix.

13. **Bundle experiment**
    > Try bundle pricing "any 2 A4 devotional for ₹1,299". Draft the landing page + cart-level promo copy. Open PR.

## Competitor / market

14. **Competitor scan**
    > Scrape Postery, ArtPix, Kraftly. What did they add in the last 14 days? Where are they beating us? Recommend 3 counter-moves.

15. **Price sanity**
    > Pull competitor prices. Are we above or below the market median for each size? One-page report.

## Housekeeping

16. **Broken things**
    > Fetch Supabase products. List every product still marked is_placeholder=true. Propose real hero images (I'll generate them separately).

17. **Review moderation**
    > Fetch reviews from the last 30 days. Draft thank-you replies for 5-star. Flag any 1-2 star for my personal reply.

18. **Sitemap + robots audit**
    > Fetch sitemap.xml and robots.txt. Flag anything stale or wrong. Propose fixes.

## Strategic

19. **Weekly executive brief**
    > It's Friday. Produce the weekly brief. Include revenue vs last week, top winning + losing pages, hypothesis verdicts, and 3 decisions for me to make.

20. **Kill list**
    > Read decisions.jsonl for entries ≥14 days old. Mark verdicts. Recommend what to kill vs double-down on.
