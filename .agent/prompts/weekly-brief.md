# Weekly Brief Prompt (Sunday)

Bigger scope than daily. Includes:

1. Pace review: are we tracking to ₹15k? What's the projected end-of-90-day number based on current trajectory?
2. Top 5 winners this week (by revenue) — deep-dive: why did they win?
3. Top 5 laggards — deep-dive: what's blocking them?
4. Competitor changes: did any competitor launch a new product, drop prices, or start ranking on our terms?
5. Content pipeline: what's shipped, what's queued, what's stuck.
6. Human-todo aging report: any open issues >7 days old? Escalate priority.
7. Reflection: read last 7 entries in `decisions.jsonl`. Which hypotheses were correct? Update `focus.md` recommendations for human.
8. Send full brief via email (if `agent-full-report.yml` wired to Resend) AND write to `.agent/reports/YYYY-WW.md`.
